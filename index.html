Who Am I?



I am a French entrepreneur and technology manager, residing in Singapore with my family.
I started my career as a software developer and project manager in the Telecom industry. In 1999, I became a mobile Internet Technology Leader, then in 2003 Software Director for a payment solution MNC.

In 2006, I switched to entrepreneurship, when I joined as co-founder Zyken, a startup in the e-Health/Wellness sector. After Zyken was acquired by Withings in 2013, I started my second company, Top Hat Voyage, which provides digital services to the Hospitality industry.

I hold a Bsc of Engineering from Ecole Centrale de Lyon and a MBA from INSEAD.

I bring Innovation Opportunities to the Market.

As an Entrepreneur and Technology Expert, I understand Businesses and how new technologies can address Customers Needs. I define Efficient Solutions, and drive Development Teams.
